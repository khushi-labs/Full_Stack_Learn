Introduction to Patna

Patna is the capital city of the Indian state of Bihar. It is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, with a rich historical and cultural heritage.


Patna has been the center of many ancient Indian empires, and its influence dates back thousands of years. The city is known for its vibrant culture, historical landmarks, and bustling markets.

Famous Places in Patna

Some famous landmarks in Patna include:

  • Golghar - A historical granary with a beautiful view of the city.
  • Patna Sahib Gurudwara - A sacred pilgrimage site for Sikhs.
  • Mahavir Mandir - A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman.

Patna also offers a unique blend of old and new, where modern developments coexist with historical traditions.
